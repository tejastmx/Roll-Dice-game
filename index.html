INSTRUCTIONS

  • The Dice Game is based on a two-player.
  • When the players click on the roll button score gets updated.
  • Both players roll the dice and the player who gets the highest score will win the game.
  • All you have to do is roll the dice and try to reach score 50 faster than your opponent player.
  • You need to complete game within one minute. If you fail to complete within the time then the player having highest score will win the game

  • if player comes on red area then score will be reduced by 5

  • if player comes on green area then score will be increased by 5
